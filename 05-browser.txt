CHECKLIST FOR ICEWEASEL BROWSER

When you go to Youtube, videos are blocked UNTIL you decide to play them.

Go to Edit -> Preferences -> Privacy.  It should be set to never remember history.

Log in to your Google Groups account, which saves your password by default.
Close all Iceweasel windows and then re-enter Iceweasel.  Go back to Google
Groups.  Your password should NOT be saved.
